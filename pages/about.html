About Alpine Robotics

Engineering with a passion.

We are FTC Team #25710 from West Windsor. Our goal: inspire 500+ students in the next three years through robotics, mentorship, and community outreach.

What drives us

Our culture centers on students, community, and iteration.

Students first

We grow builders, programmers, drivers, and leaders in a safe, inclusive space.

Community impact

Workshops in local schools, library demos, and STEM events to broaden access.

Iterate and learn

Rapid prototyping, post-match reviews, and data-driven improvements every week.

Our mission pillars

Build competitive robots, mentor new teams, and expand STEM visibility throughout the community.

Competition excellence

Disciplined build cycles, scouting, and autonomous consistency to stay competitive.

Education & outreach

Robotics demos, coding clinics, and mentoring rookie FTC/FLL teams.

Sustainability

Growing a pipeline of student leaders, documentation, and funding to keep the team thriving.
