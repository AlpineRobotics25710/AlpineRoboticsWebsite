Game Strategy

Auton, TeleOp, and Endgame approach for DECODE.

Auton

  • Goal: consistent park + 2–3 scored game pieces
  • Sensors: AprilTags, IMU, distance sensors
  • Path: preplanned + on-the-fly corrections

TeleOp

  • Cycle strategy: rapid intake + score high
  • Subsystem use: intake + lift + manipulator
  • Driver practice: weekly drills + cycle timing

Endgame

  • Scoring breakdown: endgame multipliers first
  • Priorities: secure RP, safe park, bonus tasks
