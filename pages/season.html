DECODE: building smarter, faster cycles.

Strategy-first approach: reliable auton, faster teleop cycles, and robust endgame. We’re pairing strong drive practice with rapid iteration on mechanisms.

Season priorities
• Consistent autonomous paths on both alliances • Driver practice regimen 3x weekly with metrics • Modular subsystem swaps for mid-season upgrades • Community demos to recruit and inspire

Roadmap

Milestones for DECODE — from kickoff to competition.

Kickoff & strategy

Game analysis, point/value mapping, initial prototypes.

Prototype sprints

Drivebase shakedown, intake and scoring prototypes, auton pathfinding.

Build freeze v1

Lock drivebase + main scoring, start auton consistency testing.

Scrimmages

Cycle time targets, scouting practice, refine pit checklists.

Qualifiers

Match data review, reliability upgrades, presentation polish.
